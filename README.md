# Towards High Fidelity Face Swapping: A Comprehensive Survey and New Benchmark

This repository hosts the project page for our paper:

**Towards High Fidelity Face Swapping: A Comprehensive Survey and New Benchmark**

## Overview

Face swapping aims to transfer the identity of a source face onto a target while preserving target-specific attributes such as pose, expression, illumination, and background. This project accompanies our survey and benchmark paper, which provides a unified taxonomy of existing methods and introduces **CASIA FaceSwapping**, a high-quality benchmark designed for fair and fine-grained evaluation.

## Highlights

- A comprehensive survey of face swapping methods
- A unified taxonomy covering five major paradigms
- **CASIA FaceSwapping**, a high-quality benchmark for evaluation
- Standardized protocols for:
  - Normal setting
  - Cross-ethnicity generalization
  - Cross-attribute robustness
- Additional qualitative comparisons and benchmark results
